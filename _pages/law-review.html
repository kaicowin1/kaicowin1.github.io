RSLS Law Review

The Rice Sports Law Review is an undergraduate law review that seeks to analyze legal cases and proceedings in Sports Law. As a result of the multiple interests of our members, our law review covers a wide range of legal fields - ranging from contracts, torts, and intellectual property.
